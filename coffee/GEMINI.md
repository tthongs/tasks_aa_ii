## Simple and Easy Coffee Recipes for Home

Here is a collection of simple and easy-to-make coffee recipes from highly talked-about and reputed sites, suitable for a bachelor:

### From The Kitchn:

*   **Hot Simply Good Coffee**
    *   **Ingredients:** 8 ounces brewed coffee, 1/2 to 1 ounce syrup (your choice), 1 ounce half and half/milk/alternative milk.
    *   **Instructions:** Combine ingredients in your favorite mug and stir. Add more syrup to taste.
*   **Cold Brew Concentrate**
    *   **Ingredients:** Coarsely ground coffee (1 ounce per 8 ounces water), clean, cool filtered water.
    *   **Instructions:**
        1.  Combine ground coffee and water in a large, nonreactive vessel, stirring to fully saturate.
        2.  Cover and let stand at room temperature for 8-12 hours (or longer for larger batches), ideally chilling.
        3.  Strain through a fine-mesh strainer lined with cheesecloth or a large coffee filter. Avoid pressing grounds.
        4.  Refrigerate concentrate in an airtight container for up to seven days.
        5.  To serve: Fill a glass with 1 cup of ice, add 1/2 cup cold water and 1/2 cup coffee concentrate. Stir. Dilute to taste with water, milk, or milk substitute.

### From Nespresso USA:

*   **Simple Iced Coffee (Nespresso's signature recipe)**
    *   **Instructions:** (Typically involves brewing Nespresso over ice and adding milk/sweetener. Specific instructions vary by Nespresso machine and pod type, but the principle is simple: brew over ice.)
*   **Iced Coffee with Nespresso Vertuo (Basic)**
    *   **Instructions:** Brew a Nespresso Vertuo coffee capsule, cool slightly, then add ice cubes, milk of choice (e.g., oat milk), and optional sweetener like maple syrup.
*   **Classic Iced Nespresso Espresso**
    *   **Ingredients:** Nespresso espresso shot (e.g., Ispirazione Ristretto or Intenso pod), ice, optional splash of whole milk/oat milk, optional teaspoon of simple syrup.
    *   **Instructions:** Brew espresso over ice. Add milk and simple syrup if desired.

### From Serious Eats:

*   **Cold Brew Iced Coffee**
    *   **Ingredients:** 170g (6 ounces) whole coffee beans (coarsely ground), 1,020g (36 ounces; ~4 1/4 cups) cold water.
    *   **Instructions:**
        1.  Combine ground coffee and water in a nonreactive vessel, stir to saturate.
        2.  Cover and let stand at room temperature for 8-12 hours.
        3.  Strain through cheesecloth/coffee filter.
        4.  Refrigerate concentrate.
        5.  To serve: Pour concentrate over ice and dilute to taste (starting with 1:2 concentrate to liquid ratio).

### From BBC Good Food:

*   **Dalgona Coffee (Whipped Coffee)**
    *   **Ingredients:** Equal parts instant coffee, sugar, and hot water; milk.
    *   **Instructions:** Whip instant coffee, sugar, and hot water together until frothy. Serve over milk.

### From Morgan Drinks Coffee:

*   **Hot Simply Good Coffee** (Same as The Kitchn's recipe)
    *   **Ingredients:** 8 ounces brewed coffee, 1/2 ounce to 1 ounce syrup (your choice), 1 ounce half and half/milk/alternative milk.
    *   **Instructions:** Combine ingredients in your favorite mug and stir. Add more syrup to taste.
*   **The Pantry Latte**
    *   **Ingredients:** 1/2 oz honey, pinch of ground cinnamon, pinch of ground nutmeg, double espresso, 8 oz steamed milk, orange peel for garnish.
    *   **Instructions:**
        1.  In your serving glass, add honey, then freshly pulled espresso.
        2.  Sprinkle spices, then stir.
        3.  Steam and pour latte.
        4.  Express orange peel over drink before serving.
